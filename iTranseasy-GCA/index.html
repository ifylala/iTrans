About Us


iTranseasy is an online transport fare and booking exchange. platform where commutters view transports fares to unknown destinations and different points they can pick means of transportation of their choices. Transport Operators also exchange transport fares and booking services.

We have made it easier for commutters to stay at home, offices or anywhere to access Transport fares and booking services whenever and however they want. Once you Sign Up/Sign in on the platform, input your current location and desination, select available means of transportation, you will get information on how to reach your destination safely with prices estimate.
